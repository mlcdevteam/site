Andrew Whaley
MLC, Founder/President

Andy is a pastor, professor, and entrepreneur with over 17 years of experience in Christ-centered Discipleship, nonprofit faith-based organizational leadership, pastoral counseling, men’s discipleship, and entrepreneurial leadership mentoring. Andy received his Master of Divinity from Concordia Seminary in St. Louis in 2005, and he earned his Ph.D. in Systematic Theology in 2019. His research field focuses on human flourishing through the holistic integration of beauty, goodness, and truth as it is embodied and embedded in the everyday life of the disciple of Jesus Christ. Andy’s passion is to inspire, equip, and empower leaders to advance whole-life wellness through the Gospel of Jesus Christ in the midst of a broken world!
