Mr. Dave oster
MLC, Board Chair

Dave is an MBA with over 30 years of business experience leading teams in multiple business silos. He has served as Chief Revenue Officer, Vice President, General Manager and is currently Managing Partner at the Country’s premier beauty sales agency. Dave’s omni channel role has him touching all aspects of the business; in addition to running the day to day operations of the beauty brokerage firm, he is also the founding partner of a social media business built on user-generated positive content that exists to unite and inspire a community of shared values. Dave’s passion is to cultivate teams with an attitude of creativity in finding solutions to problems and asking, “Why can’t we do that?"
